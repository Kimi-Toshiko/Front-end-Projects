FAQ

How many team members can I invite?

You may invite any amount of team members, and for each newcomer you will receive 2GB for free.

What is the maximum file upload size?

No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit to your allotted storage space.

How do I reset my password?

  1. Go to the 'Settings' tab of the website.
  2. At the top click 'Change Password' button.
  3. Type in the old password and new one.
  4. Click 'Save' button.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. You may do it anytime at your 'Profile' tab by clicking the button 'Cancel Subscription' at the very end.

Do you provide additional support?

Yes. You may contact us any time We will provide you with answers ASAP.
